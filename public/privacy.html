Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

The Executive ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using The Executive (the "App"), whether on the web, iOS, or any other platform we offer, you agree to the practices described here.

The Executive is a personal dashboard for tracking wealth, health, and daily performance. Because of what it does, it necessarily handles sensitive information you choose to enter — including financial details and health-related data. We take that responsibility seriously.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that's necessary to provide the App's functionality. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate and improve the App. Specifically:

  • To display your dashboard, calculations, and history back to you
  • To power the AI Advisor feature, which uses your dashboard data (with your account) to generate personalized responses via Anthropic's Claude API
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account status
  • To send you account-related emails (welcome, billing, password reset)
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

We do not use your data to serve you ads, and we do not sell or rent your information to third parties.

Third-Party Services We Use

The App relies on the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing for web subscriptions
  • Apple (via RevenueCat) — payment processing for iOS subscriptions
  • Anthropic — powers the AI Advisor and morning briefing features
  • Vercel — application hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Yahoo Finance and Finnhub — live market data (no personal data is shared with these services beyond the ticker symbols you choose to track)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our Supabase database, protected by row-level security policies that ensure only you can access your own data. All connections to the App are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems, with the exception of records we're legally required to retain (such as billing records, kept for tax and accounting purposes).

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Access and export your data at any time from within the App (Profile → Export Data)
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App (Profile → Delete Account)
  • Update or correct your information directly within the App

If you're located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or a jurisdiction with similar data protection laws, you may have additional rights under applicable law (such as GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. If you're located in Australia, we handle your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Children's Privacy

The Executive is not directed at, and is not intended for use by, anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@the-executive.vip.
