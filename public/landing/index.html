Questions

Straight answers.

Is my financial data safe?
All your data is stored in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device. The only external connection is when you use AI features, which sends your query to Anthropic's API. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data sold.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Add it to your home screen from Safari or Chrome and it runs like a native app — full screen, no browser chrome, works offline. Available on iPhone and Android.
What does the AI Advisor actually do?
It has full visibility of your dashboard — net worth, tasks, goals, habits, supplements and more. Ask it to review your week, suggest investments, analyse your debt strategy, or get a live market briefing with web search.
How does the recipe generator work?
It reads your health goals and body metrics, then generates personalised recipes with full ingredients and steps. Adjust servings and all amounts scale automatically. Download a beautiful interactive shopping list to take to the supermarket.
What countries are supported?
The dashboard works globally — no country-specific features required. Currency, market data and AI responses adapt to your location automatically.
Can I export my data?
Yes. From the Profile page you can export any section — net worth history, transactions, workouts, journal entries and more — as a CSV file.
