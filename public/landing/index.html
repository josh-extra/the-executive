Questions

Frequently asked

Is my data private and secure?
Yes. Your data is stored in your own Supabase account and synced securely to the cloud. AI Advisor questions are sent to Anthropic's API. Nothing is shared, sold or used for advertising. Your data is yours.
What's included in the free version?
The free version is genuinely useful and never expires — it includes tasks & habits, goals with checkpoints, journal, reading tracker, workout log, body tracking, bills & cash flow, debt calculator, and your wealth snapshot. There are no time limits, no credit card required, and no features removed after a period. Use it for as long as you like.
What do I get when I upgrade to The Executive?
Upgrading unlocks all AI-powered features: the AI Advisor (with full dashboard visibility and web search), Morning & Evening Briefing, AI goal suggestions, AI supplement recommendations, AI workout plan generator, weekly AI performance review, bank statement PDF import, and the Invest intelligence page. You also get live market prices with up to 5 custom tickers.
What is the founding member price?
The first 100 users lock in $19/month forever — even after we increase the price to $29/month. This is our way of rewarding early adopters who help build the product.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You keep full access until the end of your billing period, then drop back to the free version. All your data — tasks, journal, goals, workouts, everything — is kept intact. You never lose your history, just the AI-powered features.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from the Profile page with one click. No questions asked.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The Executive is a Progressive Web App — open it in Safari or Chrome on your phone and tap "Add to Home Screen" for a full app experience with no app store required.
What markets and assets can I track?
ASX stocks (e.g. CBA.AX), US stocks (e.g. NVDA), crypto (BTC-USD, ETH-USD), commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil), superannuation, property, alternative assets and more. Prices update live every 60 seconds.
How does the AI Advisor work?
The AI Advisor is powered by Claude (Anthropic) and has full visibility of your dashboard — your goals, habits, tasks, finances and health data. You can ask it for a full review, market insights, habit recommendations or anything else. It also has access to live web search.
