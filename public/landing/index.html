Questions

Straight answers.

Is my financial data safe?
All your data is stored in your browser's local storage — it never leaves your device. The only external connection is when you use AI features, which sends your query (not your raw financial data) to Anthropic's API. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data sold.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. The dashboard is fully responsive and works on iOS and Android browsers. For the best experience on mobile, add it to your home screen — it runs like a native app.
What does the AI Advisor actually do?
It has full visibility of your dashboard — your net worth, tasks, goals, habits, supplements and more. Ask it to review your week, suggest investments, analyse your debt strategy, or get a live market briefing. It uses web search for current market data.
Can I export my data?
Yes. From the Profile page you can export any section — net worth history, transactions, workouts, journal entries and more — as a CSV file.
How does PDF bank statement import work?
Drop your PDF onto the Cash Flow page. Claude reads the statement, extracts every transaction, categorises it automatically, and shows you a review list. You confirm what to import. It works with most Australian, US and UK bank formats.
What countries are supported?
Australia, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, New Zealand, Singapore and Germany — with correct currency symbols and local terminology. Australian users also get the tax bracket estimator for EOFY planning.
